7 web design trends you need to know

    Many tech elements that gained popularity in the latter part of 2018 continue to emerge as trends that you should know in 2019.

    The Message

  • Color Branding
    • Previously, many brands and designers were stuck with web-safe colors. More designers are becoming couragous in their approach to color, including supersaturing and vibrant shades combined with headers that are no longer just horizontal but re-imagined with slashes and hard angles.

  • Serifs on screen
    • Because of its clean readability, Sans is more favourable in various website interface. Yet, more and more brands are shifting towards bold serifs in their designs.

  • Black-and-white palettes
    • Color is what sets the mood when you first visit a blog. It defines a brand and creates visual landmarks in support to guide users through an interface.

  • Outlined type
    • In a world where chunky sans serifs dominate branding a visually lighter letterform certainly does have a different feel.

  • Mobile first
    • Mobile users have unquestionably surpassed desktop users in Kenya. Websites that are not mobile responsive, and adapting to various mobile devices across different platforms, will definitely lose out on a good number of clients.

  • Chatbots evolve
    • Along with artificial intelligence and machine learning development, chatbots at present are more intelligent and efficient in understanding customer's need.

  • Bold Typography
    • Big and bold typography on homepages make a great impression. This works best when the rest of the page is kept minimal and clean.
